AI Chatbot System

Advanced NLP chatbot using transformer models for customer service automation with 95% accuracy in intent recognition and multi-language support.

Role

Lead Data Scientist

Duration

6 months

Team Size

5 members

Impact

40% cost reduction

Project Overview

Developed an intelligent chatbot system for a major e-commerce platform to handle customer inquiries automatically. The system uses state-of-the-art transformer models to understand customer intent and provide accurate, contextual responses in real-time.

The chatbot handles over 10,000 conversations daily across multiple channels including web chat, mobile app, and social media platforms. It successfully resolves 85% of customer queries without human intervention, significantly reducing response time and operational costs.

The system incorporates advanced features like sentiment analysis, conversation context management, and seamless handoff to human agents when needed. It supports multiple languages and can be easily extended with new intents and responses.

Technical Implementation

Architecture

Microservices architecture deployed on AWS with separate services for NLP processing, intent classification, response generation, and conversation management.

NLP Pipeline

Custom BERT-based model fine-tuned on domain-specific data for intent classification. Used spaCy for text preprocessing and entity extraction.

Response Generation

Hybrid approach combining rule-based responses for common queries and GPT-based generation for complex, contextual responses.
